Chapter 42: Mosaic

The board is filled with clues, the clues tell you how many squares around the clue are black, including the square of the clue itself, the total squares around the clue are 9 in the middle of the board, 4 in the corners and 6 along the edge.

A click does a step in the circle of modes: unmarked -> marked -> blank.

A right click (or a long press on Android) does two steps instead of one.

Drag is supported, vertically and horizontally (diagonally is not), sets all unmarked tiles to the the first tile state (according to the button). Right drag might not work on touchscreens and touchpads.

The puzzle is guaranteed to be solvable, using single clues alone (the advanced logic, which works with more than one clue, is not implemented yet), the solution is not guaranteed to be unique, but it's very likely is. Anyway, a correct solution is always accepted. The game is also known as: ArtMosaico, Count and Darken, Cuenta Y Sombrea, Fill-a-Pix, Fill-In, Komsu Karala, Magipic, Majipiku, Mosaico, Mosaik, Mozaiek, Nampre Puzzle, Nurie-Puzzle, Oekaki-Pix, Voisimage. Implementation is loosely based on https://github.com/mordechaim/Mosaic

12.2 Mosaic parameters

The options available from the ‘Custom...’ option on the ‘Type’ menu are:

Width, Height
Size of grid in squares.
Level
Difficulty level, changes the amount of redundant clues, 0 means no redundant clues with 3 attempts of generating the minimum amount of clues in the board.
Advanced
This is an unsupported parameter added to prevent this version from loading future save files that will be unsolveable for it.
